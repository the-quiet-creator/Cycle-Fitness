Seminars

We at Cycle Fitness don’t just sell bikes, we also hold seminars to educate important aspects of biking.  We have a cycle of 5 seminars that occur several times throughout the year.  Below are brief descriptions of each, and how often they occur. (Note: Prices, Dates, and Times are subject to change.  Please check our calendar page for our most up-to-date schedule.)  


Bike Safety

Duration: 2 hours (including a 5 minute break in the middle)

Age range: All Ages

Cost: 3$ (Free for children 12 and under.)

Occurs: First Friday of the month


At Cycle Fitness, your safety is important to us!  That’s why every month we hold family friendly seminars on bike safety.  We will cover basic bike safety tips, as well as specific tips for biking in the big city and in the country.  We will go over rules all bikers follow, and the consequences of biking safely.  Cost is 3$, though to make sure your child knows how to bike safely, all children twelve and under can come absolutely free.  



Choosing The Right Bike

Duration: 1 Hour

Age Range: All Ages

Cost: Free!

Occurs:  Second Friday of the month

Here at Cycle Fitness, we want you to find a bike that suits you.  That is why we host FREE seminars on choosing the right bike.  In this seminar, we explain in simple terms the differences between the different kinds of bikes we offer, as well as why choosing the right bike is important to your comfort and safety.  This Seminar is free and is open for all ages.



Cycling for Fitness

Age Range: All Ages

Cost: 5$

Occurs: Third Friday of the month

Bicycling is not only a cheap, environmentally friendly way to travel.  We at cycle fitness also believe that cycling is the best form of exercise!  In this seminar, we discuss the benefits cycling for fitness can bring you, and help you set reasonable goals for you and your family!  We also discuss future trips we offer both in and out of the city.  Cycling for Fitness occurs on the 21st of each month, and costs 5$ for all ages.  For information on our trips, click here to visit our trips page!


Bike Maintenance

Duration: 1 Hour

Cost: 5$

Occurs: Fourth Friday of the month

While we offer amazing deals on a wide selection of bikes, we at Cycle Fitness realize that bikes are still really expensive.  That’s why we decided to introduce our newest seminar, the bike Maintenance seminar!  Starting in 2015, we will be hosting a Bike Maintenance seminar on the second Monday of each month.  During this seminar, we will teach new and experienced bikers how to properly maintain their bikes.  This hour long seminar costs 5$ and lasts about an hour.  Since Bike maintenance can require the use various tools, we require that all children twelve and younger be accompanied by an adult.
