What are the best ways to avoid getting into an accident with a car?

There are a lot of ways!

  • Always want to ride with the flow, never against
  • If it is night time and you are riding, always have some sort of light or reflector on to keep from getting hit from behind
  • Take a whole lane if it is absolutly needed
  • Avoid the busy streets if possible, back roads are sometimes the difference between life and death
  • If you are unsure if it is safe or not, never take the chance!
